Where to buy books

There are many places to buy and rent books. All you need is the ISBN number or title, author, and version of the book you are looking for, enter it on any (or all) of these sites. Click the Picture to go directly to the sites!

  • TextSurf

    This website is great because it compares and shows prices of all the top competitors, often times you end up going to amazon or chegg from here.

  • Chegg

    If you are looking to rent, this is the website for you. Simply save the box you got your books in and ship them back the same way without the worry of postage. They often times include gifts when you first start renting from them.

  • Amazon

    If you want fast shipping, Amazon Prime with a student account is the way to go. They often have the best rates and fast delivery for when you wait too long to order books.

My Husky

Once you enter your username and password, click Student Center to view your schedule, register for classes, make payments and more. Important information can be found here such as viewing trandscripts (GPA), "To Do List", check enrollment dates and to view any Holds on your account.

BOLT

Bloomsburg Online Learning and Teaching is where you go to recieve and submit coursework. Professors often post information and updates along with class notes. It is also where most professors require you to turn in assignments on the dropbox instead of printing it out.
